## What You Can Extract

ipinfaux provides comprehensive IP intelligence data:

- **Geolocation**: City, state, country, postal code, timezone, and coordinates
- **Privacy Detection**: VPN, proxy, Tor, hosting, and relay detection
- **ASN Information**: Autonomous system details, ASN type, and route information
- **Abuse Contacts**: Abuse email, phone, address, and network information
- **Domain Data**: Hosted domains count and domain list
- **Tags**: Detailed tags for CDN, hosting, cloud, mobile, and more
- **Other Providers**: Compare results with other geolocation providers
